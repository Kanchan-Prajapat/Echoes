## Rules

Every sprint should answer four questions:

1. What did we build?
2. Why did we build it?
3. What did we learn?
4. What's next?



# Sprint 0
Date: 2 July 2026

## Today

Today Echoes officially began.

The idea started with a simple thought.

Instagram Story Highlights have become a place where people revisit
their happiest memories.

But those memories live inside someone else's platform.

I wanted a place where my memories belonged only to me.

Not a social media application.

Not another gallery.

A personal memory vault.

That idea became Echoes.

---

## Biggest Decision

Echoes will not compete with Instagram.

Instead, it will focus entirely on helping people preserve and
relive meaningful moments.

---

## Lesson

A good product starts with a clear purpose, not with code.
